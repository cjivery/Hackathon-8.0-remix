This is your guide to using the app:

  • Find the plant you want to identify and analyze.
  • Take a picture of the plant — you'll see its description, diagnosis, and possible solutions.
  • Tap the Save button to keep the photo and analysis in your History.
  • View all your saved pictures in the History section.
  • To edit your profile, tap the icon on the top right corner.
  • Use the dropdown menu on the top left to go to the Community section where you can:
    • Share your plant pictures in blog-style posts
    • View other users’ posts to help spread awareness
  • If you need a refresher, visit the Tutorial section anytime.
